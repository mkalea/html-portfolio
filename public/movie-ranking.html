The Best Movies According to Mika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

I love this movie. is beautiful because it delicately captures the bittersweet nature of love and memory, illustrating how both the joyful and painful moments shape our identities. Its imaginative storytelling and emotional depth invite us to reflect on the impermanence of relationships and the significance of cherishing even the imperfect parts of life.

2. Wild Robot

The movie is beautiful because it showcases the delicate relationship between a robot and the animals of the wilderness, highlighting themes of empathy and survival. The story is heartwarming especially for someone like me who appreciates the beauty of animals and the natural world.

3. Interstellar

Interstellar is impressive because it shows how time moves slower near a black hole, which is based on real science, a concept rooted in Einstein's theory of relativity. The movie makes this idea easy to understand while also delivering a powerful emotional story.
